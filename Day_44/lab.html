Fun Facts of Bailey

Labradors have webbed toes and are therefore great swimmers. This webbing can also effectively act as a snowshoe in colder climates, as it keeps snow from collecting between their toes.

Their coat has two layers: a short, thick topcoat and a relatively water-resistant undercoat. This two-layer coat protects them from cold and wet weather. They are the most commonly used breed for guide dogs. They are intelligent, easy to train, eager to please and have a gentle temperament.Labradors are the most popular dog breed in America & the UK.This breed loves to be around people and needs a lot of affection, as well as plenty of exercise to burn off all that energy!
